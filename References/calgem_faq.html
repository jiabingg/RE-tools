CalGEM Mission

CalGEM regulates California's oil, gas, and geothermal industries with a focus on public health, safety, and environmental protection through science and sound engineering. CalGEM has jurisdiction over more than 242,000 wells, including nearly 101,300 active or idle oil producers, from onshore to three miles offshore. The Legislature created what is now CalGEM in 1915.
